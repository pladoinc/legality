Corporate By-law

1. Definitions

In this by-law, unless the context otherwise requires:

  • "Act" means the Not-for-Profit Corporations Act, 2010 (Ontario) and, where the context requires, includes the regulations made under it, as amended or re-enacted from time to time;
  • "Board" means the board of directors of the Corporation;
  • "By-laws" means this by-law (including the schedules to this by-law) and all other by- laws of the Corporation as amended and which are, from time to time, in force;
  • "Chair" means the chair of the Board;
  • "Corporation" means the legal entity of PLADO Inc.;
  • "Director" means an individual occupying the position of director of the Corporation by whatever name he or she is called;
  • "Donation" means a benefit provided by a third-party for the use of the Corporation;
  • "In-kind Donation" means a Donation provided by a third-party for the use of the Corporation in the form of an unpaid good or service;
  • "Member" means a member of the Corporation;
  • "Members" means the collective membership of the Corporation;
  • "Officer" means an officer of the Corporation which is defined as a President, Chair, secretary, treasurer, or Director;
  • "President" means an individual holding the office of the President of the Corporation by whatever name he or she is called; and
  • "Teacher" means an individual occupying the position of teacher of the Corporation by whatever name he or she is called.
